CS:GO

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO) is a multiplayer first-person shooter video game developed by Valve and Hidden Path Entertainment. It is the fourth game in the Counter-Strike series.

Valorant

Valorant is a free-to-play multiplayer first-person shooter developed and published by Riot Games, for Microsoft Windows.

Call of Duty: Mobile

Call of Duty: Mobile is a free-to-play first-person shooter developed by TiMi Studios and published by Activision for Android and iOS.

League of Legends

League of Legends is a multiplayer online battle arena video game developed and published by Riot Games for Microsoft Windows and macOS.

Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox video game developed by Mojang Studios. Minecraft was created by Markus "Notch" Persson in the Java programming language and was released as a public alpha for personal computers in 2009.

PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds

PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds is an online multiplayer battle royale game developed and published by PUBG Corporation.
